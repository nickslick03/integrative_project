Good A News

Greetings!

    If you or a loved one is struggling with alcoholism, the Gospel is for you and this website is for you. The following webpages contain the good news that breaks all addictions and leads to true life in Christ. The effects of Alcoholism are not only on the addicted but also on loved ones who may suffer from domestic violence due to alcohol. This unjust predicament can be made right again through the power of healing that comes from Christ’s forgiveness and sanctification.

About Me

My name is Nicholas Epps and I’m a junior at Messiah University. Alcoholism has affected my extended family and has caused all kinds of anguish and suffering. While we are still in the middle of many things, praying for healing and being there for family members has helped spread the burden of our situation.
